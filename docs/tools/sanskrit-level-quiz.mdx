# What's Your Sanskrit Level?

Six quick questions, no prior knowledge assumed for the first one. You'll get a level
badge and one concrete next step — not just a score.

<LevelQuiz />

The word-recognition and transliteration questions are drawn from the site's
[corpus-frequency data](../developers/data-cards) — the actual most-common words in a
4.55-million-token Sanskrit corpus — so "common word first" isn't a guess, it's measured.

:::tip Whichever level you land on
The [Your Name in Devanagari](name-in-devanagari) tool and the
[Devanagari quiz](../users/devanagari-quiz) are good next stops for more practice before
your next class.
:::
