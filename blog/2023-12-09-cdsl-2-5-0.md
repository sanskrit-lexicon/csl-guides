The *Abhidhānacintāmaṇi* of Hemacandra (`ABCH`), a classical Sanskrit–Sanskrit thesaurus,
was added to the Cologne Digital Sanskrit Dictionaries, and the site version was raised to
**2.5.0**.

<!-- truncate -->

Two companion texts — *Abhidhānacintāmaṇipariśiṣṭa* (`ACPH`) and
*Abhidhānacintāmaṇiśiloñcha* (`ACSJ`) — were flagged to be added in the same way.

Source: [csl-newsletter, December 2023](https://github.com/sanskrit-lexicon/csl-newsletter/blob/main/december2023.md).
