**Pāṇinian references in the Monier-Williams dictionary (`MW`) were linked**, connecting
its citations of Pāṇini's grammar to their targets.

<!-- truncate -->

This is part of the broader "Dictionary to Book" effort that turns abbreviated source
references in the entries into live click-throughs — see
[Issue Taxonomy](/contributing/issue-taxonomy) for how those link targets are built.
