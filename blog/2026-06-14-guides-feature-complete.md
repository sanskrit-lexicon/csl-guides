The CDSL guides now ship three substantial additions:

- an **auto-generated dictionary catalog** built from the live front page (so it can't
  drift from the real site),
- **offline full-text search** across the whole site, and
- **B/L/A/M display screenshots** captured from the live dictionaries (Monier-Williams in
  all four modes, plus Apte, Böhtlingk-Roth, and Grassmann for comparison).

<!-- truncate -->

The [API reference](/developers/api), [encoding & transliteration](/users/encoding-transliteration)
schemes, the source [data formats](/developers/data-formats), and the
[contributor workflow](/contributing/overview) are all documented from the live site and
the `sanskrit-lexicon` repositories. See the [catalog](/dictionaries/catalog) for the full
list of 42 dictionaries.
