# Devanāgarī & the alphabet (quiz)

The Sanskrit alphabet is **arranged by the mouth** — and that same order is the order every
dictionary sorts by. Get the alphabet's structure and you get the lookup order for free. These
16 questions (beginner-friendly) cover the vowels and consonants, the five mouth positions and
*vargas*, conjuncts, and the alphabetical order, grounded in Lessons 1–3 & 7 of Charles Wikner's
*A Practical Sanskrit Introductory*.
