# Compounds practice (quiz)

Most long Sanskrit words are **compounds (samāsa)** — and most of what you fail to find in a
dictionary is a compound you haven't split. Recognising the **type** tells you *where* to split
and how to read the result. These 15 questions drill the compound classes and the lookup move,
grounded in Lessons 8 & 11 of Charles Wikner's *A Practical Sanskrit Introductory* and the
[Reading Monier-Williams](reading-monier-williams) guide.
